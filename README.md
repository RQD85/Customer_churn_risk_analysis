## 📌 What is Customer_churn_risk_analysis?

Customer_churn_risk_analysis is an application that helps businesses understand and predict when customers might leave or stop using their services. It uses smart tools to look at customer data and find patterns. This helps you act early to keep customers satisfied and reduce losses.

The system works with stored data, analyzes it with advanced language models, and answers questions about customer behavior quickly and clearly.

You don't need to be a programmer to use this. The app is designed to work smoothly once installed.

## 🔎 How Customer_churn_risk_analysis Works

The application has three main parts to help you analyze customer churn data:

### 1️⃣ Data Handling

The app uses two folders to organize your data:

- `raw/` contains the original customer datasets before any changes.  
- `processed/` stores data after it is cleaned and ready to analyze.

You can add your datasets into the `raw/` folder. The app will help prepare them.

### 2️⃣ Data Storage

Processed data is saved inside a small database called SQLite. This keeps your data organized and easy to search.

The database files are stored locally on your machine, so your data stays private.

### 3️⃣ Smart Analysis with Language Models

The heart of the app uses a method called LangChain to run Large Language Models (LLMs). These models understand natural language questions you ask about your customer data.

You can type questions like:

- "Which customers are most likely to leave in the next three months?"  
- "What common reasons do we see for customer churn?"  
- "Show trends in customer activity over the last year."

The app will generate clear answers by looking inside your stored data.
